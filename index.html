Safety & Handling

ChemCart is committed to the highest standards of chemical safety. Please review the following guidelines before handling or ordering any chemicals. Always consult the product SDS for full details.

Hazard Codes

GHS/UN: Check the product label and SDS for pictograms and hazard statements. Understand the risks before use.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Always wear lab coat, gloves, and safety goggles. Use a fume hood for volatile or hazardous chemicals.

Storage

Store chemicals in original containers, away from heat and incompatible substances. Follow SDS storage codes.

Disposal

Dispose of chemicals as per local regulations. Never pour hazardous chemicals down the drain. Use approved waste containers.

Safety Data Sheets (SDS)

Download and review the SDS for each product before use. Keep a printed copy in your lab.

Emergency? In case of exposure, fire, or spill, follow your institution's emergency procedures and contact local authorities immediately.
